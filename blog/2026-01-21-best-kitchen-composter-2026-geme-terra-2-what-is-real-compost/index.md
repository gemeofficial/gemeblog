## What Is Real Compost? (And Why It Matters)

Before comparing products, we need to define compost properly.
Composting, Scientifically Speaking, true composting is a biological process. It requires:

 - Active microorganisms (bacteria, fungi, enzymes)
 - Controlled moisture and oxygen
 - Stable temperatures
 - Time for microbial digestion, not just drying

The output of composting is:
 - Biologically active organic matter
 - Stable humus
 - Nutrients that improve soil structure and fertility

If microorganisms are not breaking down organic material, composting is not happening.
